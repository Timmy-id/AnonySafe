The AnonySafeApp ensures that the anonymousity of its users is assured 100%. That’s not all! With the AnonySafeApp users can be rest assured that their report gets to the necessary agencies who are saddled with responding rapidly to these reports.

What is AnonySafe?

AnonySafeApp is a mobile app that allows observers and victims of crimes to report these crimes anonymously without the fear of exposure or stigmatization. The AnonySafeApp collates crime data (alerts in form of calls, messages, live broadcast, videos and audio evidences) and send these data to necessary agencies who ensure that these crimes are investigated and normalcy is restored.

Our Features

Quick Response

Sending a crime alert on the anonySafeApp takes your report directly to the necessary crime-fighting agency responsible for handling such cases. we have partnered with the public complaints rapid response unit(PCRRU) to ensure that our users get instant response to a reported crime.

Privacy

privacy or anonymity is our keyword. our app has been built with high tech firewalls that ensures that every information shared with us is kept confidential and hidden from third parties. our goal is to build a trust-based mutual relationship with all of our users through ensuring that their identity is kept secured.

Accuracy

With our live location feature, users can be rest assured of crime responders locating them as accurate and fast as a drone. Our app pinpoints the last location where an alert was made and within no time at all, a response team gets to you.

Direct Connection To Security Agencies

Reporting a crime on our app is synonymous to walking into a police station to report a case. Our servers have been linked to necessary agencies, who give top priority to reports made by our users. this not only ensures that our users get instant response, we also make sure that the appropriate agency is alerted.
